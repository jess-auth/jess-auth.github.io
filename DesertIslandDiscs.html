Desert Island Discs

I've listened to Desert Island Discs on BBC Radio 4 since I was a child (and a quick Google tells me it's been on air for almost 80 years now). In each episode, a castaway is asked to select 8 music tracks (plus one book and a luxury item) they would wish to have with them if they were marooned on a desert island. I've been mulling over and refining my selection for years. Here it is as it currently stands...

  • Maxime Le Forestier San Francisco
  • First Aid Kit Waitress Song
  • Ewan Dobson I Know Your Pain
  • The Mamas and The Papas California Dreamin'
  • Revolver Leave Me Alone
  • Green Day Good Riddance (Time of Your Life)
  • Fleet Foxes Mykonos
  • Allegri Miserere mei, Deus

I think I'd take the Collected Ghost Stories of M R James as my book, and for my luxury item I'd definitely choose a pillow.
